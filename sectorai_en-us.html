The problem

Too much data.
Too few decisions.

Every race generates terabytes. Temperature, friction, camber, wind, tyre wear, engine telemetry. Engineers have the data. They don't have time to turn it into action before the window closes.

∞
variáveis por volta

Information overload

MoTeC, AiM, Bosch — every existing tool is a data visualiser. They show what happened. None tells you what to do next.

The window closes in seconds

Setup, tyre strategy and driving adjustments must be decided during the session — not on Monday, when the engineer finishes the analysis.

Fragmented data, isolated silos

Weather lives in one place. Telemetry in another. Track history, somewhere else. No current tool crosses these sources into a single actionable view.

Guesswork still exists at the top

Even F4 and F.Regional teams make decisions based on engineer intuition when time is short. That costs tenths — and tenths cost podiums.
